About NexOne

Certified by the Small Business Administration as a Small Disadvantaged Business, NexOne is a professional services company specializing in Information Technology, Engineering, and Logistics and Technical Support. It has become a leading small business provider to government, military, and federal prime contractors, through the ability to attract, manage, and retain professional talent. We focus on quality and have a record of exceptional performance and customer satisfaction.
